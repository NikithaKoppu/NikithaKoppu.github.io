Hi My name is Nikitha.

I love what I learn more each day.

A little bit about myself: I'm a 4th year CS and Cognitive Psychology major at Northeastern University aspiring to become a UX researcher and a software engineer. I primarily focus on backend engineering, but I do love to work collaboratively on all kinds of challenges. My combined major has helped me understand that I enjoy the UX design process and all its iterations as well as the more logical development environment.

Ultimately, its people who are creating the software for other people to use. With that, it's good to know how people interact with the software in order to make it more usable. Throughout my experiences both in my classes and at my internships, I have learned new things about the process of making good software and the impact of users. That has kept me excited every day to keep learning and growing.
