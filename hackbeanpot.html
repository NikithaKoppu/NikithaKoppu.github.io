HackBeanpot 2020 May 2019

In an effort to create a hackathon for novices in the tech industry. No pressure, no competition. We just want you to have fun learning!

Apart from being a student, I also am on the organizing core team for a Boston-based hackathon called HackBeanpot. We're a student-led, non-profit, independent organization and this is our 8th annual hackathon. I am on the sponsorship team, and we have raised over $20,000 in funds to put on this event for students who are new to hackathons. I've worked closely with my fellow members to craft a sponsorship packet that encompasses both the desires of our participants and companies interested.

To be able to communicate our core values and hopes for the event to sponsors has been a rewarding experience. My hope is to break down barriers between students and company reps to allow better communication and long term interaction. It's been amazing to see how this event comes together over the past year and I'm excited to see it in person in February!
