In the past year, I've gotten really interested in skincare. Without having a chemistry background though, it has been difficult to understand what all the ingredients in a product are and what their purpose is. A large portion of the cosmetics industry has yet to transition into the clean skin trend which involves not adding any chemicals or irritants to products. So I still look up the complicated ingredient list for my safety. I have accumulated enough knowledge to make better decisions for my skin but it took me reading dozens of articles. This website hopes to be a one stop shop for researching skincare products and their ingredients in a simple and digestible manner. Additionally, it would provide helpful tips on good routines and allow you to curate your own regimen.
