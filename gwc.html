GirlsWhoCode Feb 2020

In the hopes of bringing gender equality to the STEM fields...

A new endeavor of mine is mentoring young girls entering the computer science field. All throughout highschool, I had an interest in computer science, but without a guiding mentorship program, I ended up dabbling in other areas of study until college. When I came to Northeastern, I dove headfirst into the CS program and found like-minded female students who encouraged and pushed me to explore what coding can do. Now I am a mentor myself and hope to motivate young girls to not be afraid to dive in because there will always be a community to support them.
