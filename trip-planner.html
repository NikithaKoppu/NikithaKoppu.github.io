Trip Planner Sept 2019

In an effort to keep my sanity while planning a trip, I created trip planner

While planning my spring break trip to Portugal and Spain this year, my friends and I had a lot of trouble organizing all our finalized information into one place. It was spread out across 3 different brainstorming google docs and in 4 different slack channels depending on the city. If I did not keep up with the changes in the chats, then I would have been completely lost. To resolve this problem, I mocked out an app where flight plans, itinerary, packing info, etc could be encapsulated in one place. Also it would be easily accessible in app form.
